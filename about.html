ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Vaishnavi Industries is a leading manufacturer of high‑quality process equipment for diverse industrial sectors. Our relentless focus on innovation and customer satisfaction has helped us build a trusted brand in India and beyond.

Mission: Deliver world‑class engineering solutions that boost productivity and safety for our clients.

Vision: Become a global benchmark for quality, sustainability & continuous improvement.

  • Integrity: We do the right thing, always.
  • Quality: Zero‑defect mindset in every project.
  • Innovation: Constantly seeking smarter solutions.
  • Customer‑Focus: Your success is our priority.

Our Pune facility spans 20,000 sq ft, housing CNC machining, automated welding lines and a dedicated QA/QC lab. We operate with ISO‑certified processes and an integrated ERP for real‑time tracking.

15+ years of industry experience, 300+ successful installations, and multiple awards for excellence in manufacturing and safety compliance.
